XVth Conference - AGENDA

Meetings of the "Circle of Presidents"

   

AGENDA OF THE CIRCLE OF PRESIDENTS

First session

1. Opening of the meeting by the President of the Constitutional Court of Romania.
2. Approval of the agenda.
3. Organisational issues for the XVth Congress:
          (a) chairmanship of plenary sessions;
          (b) approval of rapporteurs;
          (c) procedure of debates (presentation of reports, registration, duration of
               speeches, questions – answers etc.);
          (d) publication of reports.
4. Follow-up to the Second World Conference on Constitutional Justice: "Draft Statute
     of the World Conference on Constitutional Justice".
5. Application for admission to the Conference of European Constitutional Courts
    (associate membership).
6. Miscellanea.

 

Second session

7. Financial matters:
          (a) report on general costs of organising the Congress (provisional budget;
                allocation of expenses);
          (b) other financial decisions.
8. Resolutions of the XVth Congress – discussion and adoption.
9. Adoption of the final communiqué of the XVth Congress.
